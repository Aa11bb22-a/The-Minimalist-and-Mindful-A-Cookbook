Simple Vegetable Stir-fry

A quick and easy dish that brings out the natural flavors of your favorite vegetables.

  • Assorted Vegetables
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Instructions: Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, then vegetables, and stir-fry until cooked. Season with soy sauce and pepper.
